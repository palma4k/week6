"My Story"

I was born in Guatemela, I came to the united states when I was a child. I grew up in California, in the city of Newhall, county of Los Angeles. This city is also considered to be the city of Santa Clarita. I honestly don't remember anything about Guatemala, the last time I was there was back in June 2004, for one of my Aunt's funeral. I Haven't been there since then. I've been wanting to go back to visit family, and all the nice places Guatemala has to offer. Perhaps next year I will travel there to see how it is. I call the United States, My Country, My Home, I've served MY COUNTRY for five years and three months in the UNITED STATES MARINE CORPS. Before the Marine Corps, I was in a band that made a CD. Which allowed us to travel to Peru, Mexico, and places in Southern California. The instruments I play are; bass, drums, guitar, and keyboard. I've been to several places, for fun and for business, those are; Peru, Guatemala, Mexico, Canada, Germany, kurkistan, Afghanistan, Japan, Guam, Wake Island, and Ireland. Some of those places have some good memories, some don't and others not many. I had my first set of twins when I was 28 years old and my second set of twins when I was 30 years old. Their birthdays are: March 2011, and October 2012. I know what you might be thinking, I was a busy man, well, that's what happens when you are on active duty, and come back from a deployment.
